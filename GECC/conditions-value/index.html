Glen Eira City Council · Strategy Discovery Baseline

Conditions and Value Capture Survey

As you are aware, Glen Eira City Council is commencing a strategic planning process to consider how the organisation should evolve over the coming decade.

As part of this process, we are capturing baseline insights from across the organisation to understand: conditions shaping the environment in which Council operates, value currently delivered to residents and community, and expectations of future value from our community.

There are no right or wrong answers – please take a broad view. Your perspective will help build a richer understanding of the context Council is navigating.
